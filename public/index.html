About me

I am a PhD candidate in Statistics at Monash University, Australia. My PhD research focuses on the problem of forecasting large collections of time series. I am working with Professor Rob J Hyndman and Professor George Athanasopoulos. I am a member of ACEMS - ARC Centre of Excellence for Mathematical & Statistical Frontiers.

I enjoy solving general data science problems from three different angles: theoretical, computational, applied. In this website you will find some of my work and interests on statistics and data analysis. I am also interested in R programming language.

My sister Priyanga Dilini Talagala is also a PhD candidate at Monash University, Australia.
